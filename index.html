Test Parameters

10 Parameter Test

Standard

Comprehensive screening for common urinary conditions

  • Leukocytes - UTI indicator
  • Nitrites - Bacterial infection
  • Urobilinogen - Liver function
  • Protein - Kidney function
  • Blood - Hematuria detection
  • pH - Acidity level
  • Specific Gravity - Hydration
  • Ketones - Metabolic screening
  • Bilirubin - Liver disease
  • Glucose - Diabetes screening

14 Parameter Test with ACR

Advanced

Extended screening including early renal damage detection

  • Leukocytes (LEU) - UTI indicator
  • Urobilinogen (URO) - Liver function
  • Microalbumin (mAlb) - Early kidney damage ⭐
  • Protein (PRO) - Kidney function
  • Bilirubin (BIL) - Liver disease
  • Glucose (GLU) - Diabetes screening
  • Ascorbic Acid (ASC) - Vitamin C level ⭐
  • Specific Gravity (SG) - Hydration
  • Ketones (KET) - Metabolic screening
  • Nitrites (NIT) - Bacterial infection
  • Creatinine (CRE) - Kidney assessment ⭐
  • pH - Acidity level
  • Blood (BLO) - Hematuria
  • Calcium (CA) & ACR - Renal health ratio ⭐
ACR (Albumin-to-Creatinine Ratio): Critical for screening kidney disorders including early renal damage, especially in diabetic and hypertensive patients.
