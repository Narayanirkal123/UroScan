Product Overview

The UroScan Handheld Urine Biochemical Analyzer utilizes an advanced dual-wavelength reflectance measurement principle to deliver long service life and excellent repeatability. Designed as a portable dry biochemical analyzer, it features a built-in lithium battery, making it ideal for both indoor and outdoor testing environments.

With one-button operation and a fully automated, standardized testing process, the UroScan minimizes human error while significantly improving testing efficiency and result accuracy. Capable of providing 10 or 14 urine test parameters within just 60 seconds, this IVD analyzer is well suited for clinical laboratories, point-of-care settings and rapid urine biochemical analysis.
